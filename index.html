About Tuteshwor Nath Temple

The Tuteshwor Nath Temple, located in the heart of Bardibas, Province No. 2, is a sacred site that offers a peaceful retreat and a glimpse into the rich cultural and spiritual heritage of Nepal. Visit us to explore the temple's ancient architecture, engage in spiritual practices, and partake in vibrant local festivals.
