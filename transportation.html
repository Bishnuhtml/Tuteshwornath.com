How to Get Here

Traveling to Tuteshwor Nath Temple is convenient from various locations. Hereâ€™s how you can reach us:

  • By Air: The nearest airport is in Janakpur, approximately 50 km from Bardibas. From Janakpur, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Bardibas.
  • By Bus: Bardibas is well-connected by road. You can take a direct bus from Kathmandu or other major cities in Nepal. The journey from Kathmandu takes about 4-5 hours.
  • By Car: If you prefer driving, Bardibas is accessible via the East-West Highway (also known as Mahendra Highway). The road is well-maintained and offers a scenic drive through the Terai region.
  • Local Transportation: Once in Bardibas, you can use local taxis, auto-rickshaws, or walk to the temple. The temple is centrally located and easy to find.
